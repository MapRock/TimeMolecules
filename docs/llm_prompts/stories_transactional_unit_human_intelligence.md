Prompt: What is the most effective way humans transfer complex knowledge, experiences, and context?

Abstract: Stories (event sequences with context, challenges, decisions, and outcomes) are the fundamental, versatile unit of meaningful human communication and intelligence. Our brains naturally record, remix, and link stories—especially via the Default Mode Network. Time Molecules extracts these narrative patterns from raw event streams, turning enterprise data into shareable, memorable knowledge that LLMs and humans can reason over.
