* **Prompt**: What is the Assemblage of Artificial Intelligence, and how does it build effective enterprise intelligence in the NoLLM (Not Only Large Language Models) epoch?
* **Abstract**: This essay introduces the Assemblage of Artificial Intelligence as a hybrid architecture for the NoLLM epoch of the LLM Era. Intelligence emerges not from a single monolithic model but from the dynamic interplay of specialized components working in symbiosis: knowledge graphs for structured memory and semantic relationships, Prolog-style logic engines for transparent reasoning, causation, and deductive inference, business intelligence systems for capturing and analyzing temporal processes, classic machine learning for statistical pattern discovery, and large language models serving as the connective, interpretive, and creative layer. Drawing an analogy to successful businesses that convert streams of events into memory, stories, procedures, and adaptive plans, the work shows how this assemblage produces reusable artifacts of intelligence that are auditable, scalable, and human-grounded. The page functions as the central hub and curated table of contents for the third volume in the Enterprise Intelligence trilogy, providing a practical blueprint for enterprise AI that augments collective capability while preserving human agency.
